6) Employment Terms
Chunk 16: a) Probation Period - All full-time employees will undergo a 90-day P bation period, during which your performance will be assessed regularly. Successful completion of this probation is essential for your continued employment at Siam Computing.
Chunk 17: b) Notice Period - For full-time employees, a 60-day notice period is required from the date of resignation. Employees who are still in their probation period are required to serve a 14-day notice.
Chunk 18: c) Working Days - Our standard working days are Monday through Friday. Additionally, every second Saturday of the month is designated as a Learning & Development day.
Chunk 19: 7) Workplace Policies
a) Leave Policy:
Chunk 20: Confirmed employees are entitled to a total of 20 days of leave per year, excluding government holidays. These 20 days are categorized as follows:
Chunk 21: Privilege/Planned Leave: 12 days per year
Chunk 22: Sick/Casual Leave: 2 days per quarter (8 days annually)
Chunk 23: During the probation period, employees are eligible for 1 day of leave per month.
Chunk 24: A list of declared holidays will be shared by the company at the beginning of each year.
Chunk 25: b) Hybrid Work Policy:
Chunk 26: Non-Tech Teams: Must follow assigned work schedules and report to the office as required.
